# だい3か: 你為什麼提早起床 "どうして　はやく　おきるんですか。"

## [**かいわ 1**](https://youtu.be/7H1meOG52YY?t=90)

### **毎朝何時に起きるんですか**

Eng: "What time do you wake up every morning?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 毎朝 | mai-asu | Noun | every morning |
| 何時に | nan-ji-ni | Adverbial phrase | at what time |
| 起きる | o-ki-ru | Predicate | to wake up |
| ん | n | Auxiliary | explanatory particle |
| ですか | desu-ka | Ending | question particle for polite inquiry |


### **7時に　おきます。**

Eng: "I wake up at 7 o'clock."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 7時 | shichi-ji | Noun | 7 o'clock |
| に | ni | Particle | indicates the time at which the action occurs |
| おきます | o-ki-masu | Predicate | present tense of the verb 起きる (o-ki-ru, "to wake up") |


### **でも明日は早く起きようと思います**

Eng: "But tomorrow, I plan to wake up early."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| でも | de-mo | Conjunction | "but" |
| 明日 | ashita | Noun | "tomorrow" |
| は | wa | Particle | marks the topic of the sentence |
| 早く | ha-ya-ku | Adverb | "early" |
| 起きよう | o-ki-yo-u | Predicate | volitional form of the verb 起きる (o-ki-ru, "to wake up") |
| と | to | Particle | marks the following verb as a volitional form |
| 思います | o-mo-i-masu | Predicate | present tense of the verb 思う (o-mo-u, "to think") in polite form |


### **どうして　早く　起きるんですか**

| です | desu | Auxiliary | polite sentence ending |

### **ジョギングをするからです。**

| ジョギング | jo-gi-ngu | Object | jogging |

| です | de-su | Sentence ender | polite sentence ending |

### **じゃ、何時に　起きれば　いいですか。**

Eng: "So, what time should I wake up?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| じゃ | ja | Particle | a casual contraction of では (de wa) |
| 何時に | nan-ji-ni | Adverbial phrase | at what time |
| 起きれば | o-ki-re-ba | Conditional form | conditional form of the verb 起きる (o-ki-ru, "to wake up") |
| いいですか | ii-desu-ka | Interrogative sentence | "is it okay?" or "what do you think?" |

### **5時半に起きてください**

Eng: "Please wake up at 5:30."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5時半 | go-ji-han | Noun | 5:30 |
| に | ni | Particle | marks the time when the action of the sentence will happen |
| 起きて | o-ki-te | Verb | te-form of the verb 起きる (o-ki-ru, "to wake up") |
| ください | ku-da-sai | Auxiliary verb | "please" |


はい。

### **じゃ、おやすみなさい。**

Eng: "Good night then."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| じゃ | ja | Particle | contraction of では (de-wa), used to indicate a farewell |
| おやすみなさい | o-ya-su-mi-na-sai | Sentence | polite way to say "good night" |

おやすみなさい。
