Eng: "What is this a picture of?"

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| これ | ko-re | Subject | This |
| は | wa | Particle | Topic marker |

| 写真 | sha-shin | Object | Picture |
| ですか | desu-ka | Particle | Indicates a question |

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 箱根 | ha-kone | Adverb | Hakone |
