## [**かいわ 2**](https://youtu.be/7H1meOG52YY?t=71)

### **今度の日曜日にどこかへ行きますか**

Eng: "Are you going somewhere this coming Sunday?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 今度 | kon-do | Adverbial noun | this time; next time |
| の | no | Particle | indicates possession |
| 日曜日 | ni-chi-yo-u-bi | Noun | Sunday |
| に | ni | Particle | marks time when an action occurs |
| どこか | do-ko-ka | Pronoun | somewhere |
| へ | e | Particle | marks direction or goal of an action |
| 行きますか | i-ki-ma-su-ka | Predicate | polite question form of the verb 行く (i-ku, "to go") |


### **プールへ行こうと思います。**

Eng: "I'm thinking of going to the pool."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| プール | pu-ru | Destination | pool |
| へ | e | Particle | marks the destination of the action |
| 行こう | i-ko-u | Predicate | volitional form of the verb 行く (i-ku, "to go") |
| と | to | Particle | marks the preceding verb as a "plan" or "intention" |
| 思います | o-mo-i-masu | Predicate | polite form of the verb 思う (o-mo-u, "to think") |

### **一緒に　行きますか。**

Eng: "Would you like to go together?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 一緒に | issho-ni | Adverb | together |
| 行きます | i-ki-masu | Predicate | present tense of the verb 行く (i-ku, "to go") |
| か | ka | Particle | marks the sentence as a question |


### **いいですか。**

Eng: "Is it okay?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| いい | i-i | Adjective | good, okay |
| です | de-su | Copula | to be (polite) |
| か | ka | Particle | question marker |

### **ええ。まず、私のうちへ来てください。**

Eng: "Yes. First, please come to my house."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ええ | ee | Particle | an affirmative response |
| まず | ma-zu | Adverb | first |
| 私 | wa-ta-shi | Subject | I/me |
| の | no | Particle | indicates possession |
| うち | u-chi | Object | home |
| へ | e | Particle | indicates direction of movement |
| 来て | ki-te | Predicate | te-form of the verb 来る (ku-ru, "to come") |
| ください | ku-da-sai | Auxiliary Verb | a polite request form of the verb くれる (ku-reru, "to give") |

### **なんじに　いけば　いいですか。**

Eng: "What time should I go?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| なんじ | nan-ji | Noun | what time |
| に | ni | Particle | indicates the time when the action takes place |
| いけば | i-ke-ba | Conditional | conditional form of the verb 行く (i-ku, "to go") |
| いいですか | ii-desu-ka | Predicate | polite form of the verb いい (ii, "good") and the auxiliary verb です (desu) used to form a question |

### **11時に来てください**

Eng: "Please come at 11 o'clock."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 11時 | juu-ichi-ji | Object of the Preposition | "11 o'clock" |
| に | ni | Particle | indicates the time "11 o'clock" |
| 来てください | ki-te-kudasai | Verb Phrase | imperative form of the verb "to come" (kuru), and kudasai meaning "please"  |


### **分かりました**

Eng: "I understand"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 分かりました | wa-ka-ri-ma-shi-ta | Predicate | past tense of the verb 分かる (wa-ka-ru, "to understand") |
