### **今度の 日曜日に友達とテニスをやるんです**

Eng: "I am going to play tennis with my friend this coming Sunday."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 今度 | kon-do | Adverb | this time/coming |
| の | no | Particle | indicates possession |
| 日曜日 | ni-chi-yo-u-bi | Noun | Sunday |
| に | ni | Particle | marks the time when the action occurs |
| 友達 | to-mo-da-chi | Noun | friend |
| と | to | Particle | marks the doer of an action |
| テニス | te-ni-su | Noun | tennis |
| を | o | Particle | marks the direct object of the verb |
| やる | ya-ru | Verb | do/play |
| んです | n-desu | Sentence ending | informal explanation (short for "no desu") |


### **一緒に　やりましょう**

Eng: "Let's do it together."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 一緒に | i-ssho-ni | Adverb | together |
| やりましょう | ya-ri-ma-shou | Predicate | volitional form of the verb やる (ya-ru, "to do") |


### **何時からやるんですか**

Eng: "What time will you start?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 何時から | nan-ji-ka-ra | Adverb | from what time |
| やる | ya-ru | Predicate | to do |
| ん | n | Auxiliary verb | short for の (no) |
| ですか | de-su-ka | Particle | question particle |


### **よじから 6時まで やるんです**

Eng: "We're playing tennis from 4 pm until 6 pm."

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| よじから | yo-ji-kara | Noun phrase | "from 4 pm" |
| 6時まで | roku-ji-made | Noun phrase | "until 6 pm" |
| やるんです | ya-ru-n-desu | Predicate | present/future tense of the verb やる (ya-ru, "to do"), with explanatory ん (n) and polite sentence-final です (desu) |


### **じゃ、何時に　どこで　会えば　いいですか。**

Eng: "So, what time and where should we meet?"

| Word | Romaji | Role | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| じゃ | ja | Conjunction | casual contraction of それでは (so, well then) |
| 何時に | nan-ji-ni | Adverbial phrase | at what time |
| どこで | doko-de | Adverbial phrase | at where |
| 会えば | a-e-ba | Conditional | If meet |
| いいですか | ii-desu-ka | Sentence-ending | Is it okay? |
